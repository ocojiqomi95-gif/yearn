⚡ Features

What Makes Yearn
Different

Yearn Finance combines cutting-edge DeFi engineering, rigorous security, and autonomous optimization to deliver superior yields across all market conditions.

Core Technology

Engineered for
Performance

Every feature is purpose-built to maximize returns while minimizing risk for DeFi participants at every level.

🔄

Automated Rebalancing

Vault strategies continuously monitor yields across dozens of DeFi protocols. When a better opportunity appears, funds are automatically redeployed to capture higher returns — no manual intervention required.

📊

Multi-Strategy Vaults

Each vault can run multiple independent strategies simultaneously, diversifying risk while maximizing yield. The Vault Allocator manages capital distribution based on performance, risk, and capacity constraints.

🛡️

Layered Security

Yearn's contracts are protected by multiple audits from top security firms, timelocks on upgrades, multisig governance controls, and an active security committee that monitors for threats 24/7.

⛽

Gas Optimization

By pooling user deposits, Yearn amortizes Ethereum gas costs across all participants. Individual users benefit from institutional-scale gas efficiency, making high-frequency yield harvesting economically viable.

🧩

DeFi Composability

Yearn vault tokens (yvTokens) are fully composable ERC-20 tokens. Use them as collateral in lending protocols, provide liquidity in DEXes, or integrate them into your own DeFi strategies.

🌐

Multi-Chain Support

Yearn operates across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, and Polygon, giving users access to yield opportunities across the entire EVM ecosystem from a single unified interface.

🛡️ Security

Security at Every
Layer

Protecting user funds has been Yearn's top priority since day one. Our multi-layered security architecture provides industry-leading protection.

🔍

Independent Audits

All Yearn smart contracts undergo rigorous security audits from multiple independent firms before deployment. Audit reports are publicly available in the Yearn GitHub repository for full transparency.

⏱️

Timelock Controls

Governance changes are subject to timelocks that give the community time to review and respond to proposed changes. Critical upgrades require multi-day notice periods enforced at the contract level.

🔑

Multisig Governance

Protocol-level changes require approval from a multisig committee of trusted community members. No single actor can make unilateral changes to Yearn's core contracts or treasury.

🚨

Emergency Shutdown

Yearn's emergency shutdown mechanism allows guardians to halt vault operations and return funds to users in the event of a security incident, providing a last line of defense for depositors.

💰 Yield Mechanics

How Yearn
Generates Yield

🏦

Lending Protocols

Strategies supply assets to Aave, Compound, Morpho, and other lending protocols to earn base interest rates. Borrowed assets are leveraged to amplify returns when risk parameters allow.

🌊

Liquidity Provision

Capital is deployed to Curve, Balancer, and Uniswap liquidity pools to earn trading fees. CRV, BAL, and other protocol emissions are harvested and compounded back into the vault.

🎯

Yield Aggregation

The APR Oracle tool continuously tracks yields across hundreds of DeFi protocols, allowing vault strategists to identify and capture the highest-returning opportunities in real time.
