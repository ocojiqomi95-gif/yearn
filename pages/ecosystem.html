yVaults — Yield-Generating Vaults
🏦

yVaults

Live ERC-4626

yVaults are the flagship product of Yearn Finance — automated, yield-generating smart contracts that accept ERC-20 token deposits and deploy them across curated DeFi strategies. When you deposit into a yVault, you receive yvTokens representing your share of the vault's assets. These vault tokens auto-compound returns, meaning your balance grows over time without any manual harvesting. Each vault runs one or more strategies that interact with protocols like Aave, Compound, Curve, Convex, and Morpho to generate yield from lending, liquidity provision, and farming. The Vault Allocator contract dynamically distributes capital between strategies based on APY, risk parameters, and capacity limits, ensuring optimal allocation at all times.

Curation — Lending Market Curation
🎯

Curation

Lending

Yearn's Curation product focuses on intelligently allocating capital across lending markets to maximize risk-adjusted returns. Rather than deploying funds to a single lending protocol, Curated Vaults monitor dozens of lending markets simultaneously and rebalance between them as rates change. The curation system evaluates each market's interest rate, utilization ratio, asset quality, and protocol risk score to construct an optimal lending portfolio. Users benefit from institutional-grade capital allocation without needing to manage positions across multiple protocols. Curated Vaults are particularly effective for stablecoin deposits, where lending rates across platforms like Morpho, Aave, and Compound can vary significantly.

Liquid Lockers
🌊

yCRV

veCRV Liquid Locker

yCRV is Yearn's liquid wrapper for CRV tokens. Instead of locking CRV for up to 4 years to obtain veCRV, users can deposit CRV into the yCRV contract to receive yCRV — a fully liquid, tradeable ERC-20 token. Yearn maintains one of the largest veCRV positions in the Curve ecosystem, using its voting power to direct CRV emissions toward Yearn's liquidity pools and boost yields for yCRV holders. yCRV holders earn Curve admin fees (3CRV distributions), boosted CRV rewards, and a share of Yearn's governance revenue. yCRV can be staked in st-yCRV for auto-compounding returns, or used as-is in external DeFi protocols.

🔷

yYB

veYB Liquid Locker

yYB follows the same model as yCRV, applied to the Yearn Blue (YB) token ecosystem. Holders of YB tokens can deposit them into yYB to receive a liquid representation of their locked position without the traditional lock-up period. Yearn manages a strategic veYB position, using governance votes to maximize rewards and protocol emissions for yYB participants. Holders earn veYB gauge rewards, platform fees distributed by the Yearn Blue protocol, and governance participation rights. yYB is designed to be fully composable with DeFi protocols that support ERC-20 tokens, enabling additional yield opportunities beyond the base protocol rewards.

YFI Staking
💎

stYFI — YFI Staking

Live ERC-4626

stYFI is the auto-compounding ERC-4626 staking vault for Yearn's native governance token, YFI. By depositing YFI into the stYFI contract, holders earn a proportional share of Yearn's protocol revenue — fees collected from yVaults, liquid lockers, and other Yearn products are distributed to stYFI depositors. Unlike traditional staking mechanisms, stYFI implements the ERC-4626 standard, making it seamlessly composable with other DeFi protocols. Stakers can use stYFI as collateral in lending protocols, provide liquidity in DEXes, or deposit it into further yield strategies. Returns are auto-compounded, increasing the YFI value of each stYFI token over time. YFI's fixed supply of 36,666 tokens makes stYFI a high-conviction governance and yield token.

🔧 Tools

Developer & Analytics
Tools

A suite of open-source tools built by Yearn contributors to power the ecosystem's analytics, management, and vault creation workflows.

📊

PowerGlove

Analytics

PowerGlove is Yearn's advanced vault analytics platform. It provides detailed performance breakdowns for every Yearn vault, including historical APY data, strategy allocations, harvest history, and fee analytics. Designed for power users and researchers who need granular insight into vault operations.

GitHub ↗
🗄️

Kong

Data API

Kong is Yearn's comprehensive vault data service. It indexes on-chain vault data and exposes it through a clean REST API, enabling developers to build applications using reliable, up-to-date Yearn data. Kong tracks TVL, APY, strategy performance, and historical yields for all Yearn vaults across every supported chain.

GitHub ↗
⚙️

Kalani

Management

Kalani is the Vault Management Interface used by Yearn strategists and vault managers to monitor, configure, and manage vault parameters. Through Kalani, authorized users can adjust strategy allocations, update debt ratios, harvest strategies, and respond to changing market conditions with precision and speed.

GitHub ↗
🏭

yFactory

Creation

yFactory is a permissionless LP token vault creation tool. Anyone can use yFactory to deploy a new ERC-4626 vault for any Curve, Balancer, or other LP token. Vaults created through yFactory inherit Yearn's battle-tested vault architecture, standard security properties, and can immediately benefit from Yearn's existing strategy ecosystem.

GitHub ↗
🔮

APR Oracle

Yield Tool

The APR Oracle is Yearn's on-chain projected vault APY tool. It calculates expected annual returns for Yearn vaults based on current strategy allocations, protocol rates, and emission schedules. The oracle is used internally by Yearn's infrastructure and is available for external protocols to query projected yields for any supported Yearn vault.
