Beware of Scams

Yearn Finance will never DM you first on any platform. We will never ask for your private key, seed phrase, or ask you to approve transactions to "recover" funds. If you receive such messages, they are scams. Only interact with Yearn through the official app at app.yearn.fi and the official social channels listed on this page.
