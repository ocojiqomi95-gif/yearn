Yearn's Suite of
DeFi Products

From yield-generating vaults to governance tools and liquid lockers, Yearn's product suite covers every angle of DeFi participation.

Yield-Generating Products
🏦

yVaults

The core Yearn product. yVaults automatically deploy user deposits across the highest-yielding strategies available in DeFi, compounding returns and rebalancing without any user action required.

Yield Optimization
🎯

Curation — Lending Market

Yearn's curated lending market vaults intelligently allocate capital across lending protocols like Morpho and Aave, selecting markets with the best risk-adjusted rates for each asset class.

Lending Curation
Liquid Lockers
🌊

yCRV — veCRV Liquid Locker

yCRV lets users unlock the benefits of veCRV without long-term lock-ups. Deposit CRV to receive yCRV — a liquid, tradeable token that accrues Curve admin fees, gauge voting rewards, and boosted yields from Yearn's deep veCRV position.

Liquid Locker
🔷

yYB — veYB Liquid Locker

yYB provides liquidity for veYB positions in the Yearn Blue ecosystem. Users deposit YB tokens to receive yYB, gaining access to veYB yield and governance participation while retaining full token liquidity and composability.

Liquid Locker
Staking
💎

stYFI — YFI Staking

stYFI allows YFI token holders to stake and earn a share of Yearn's protocol fees. Stakers receive auto-compounding rewards while maintaining governance rights. stYFI is a liquid ERC-4626 token, making it seamlessly composable with other DeFi protocols.
